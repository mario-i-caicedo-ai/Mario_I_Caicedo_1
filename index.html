Mario I. Caicedo, PhD

Founder of Physics, Math & Coffee

"Physics is not about memorizing equations; it is about training the mind to see the underlying structure of the universe."

Welcome. I am Mario I. Caicedo, a PhD physicist, educator, and the founder of Physics, Math & Coffee. After more than 30 years in academic research and university leadership, my mission today is focused on breaking down complexity, community teaching, and building interactive tools that bring mathematical physics to life.

My path started in pure research physics, diving deep into the mathematical foundations of Field Theory. For decades, I served as a Full Professor, Department Head, and Program Coordinator at Universidad Simon Bolivar in Caracas, Venezuela, helping shape academic physics curricula.

However, true understanding doesn't belong hidden behind heavy academic walls. It belongs where the spark of curiosity is ignited. This realization led to the birth of Physics, Math & Coffee—a space designed to bridge the gap between rigorous mathematical physics and intuitive, visual logic. Today, as an instructor at Houston City College, and at Lone Star College CyFair and an independent mentor, my focus is entirely on training students to reason rather than just calculate, supporting diverse backgrounds in mastering the language of science.

Current Operations

  • 💻 Simulation Hub: Crafting real-time JavaScript vector engines to make visual physics accessible on any device.
  • 🤖 AI Verification: Applying STEM expertise to verify, stress-test, and improve advanced artificial intelligence models.
  • 🏫 Academic Focus: Faculty member at Houston City College, specialized in structural reasoning.
